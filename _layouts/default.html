I’m a Product Designer building people-centered software and platforms. Sometimes that’s an editorial platform like Apartment Therapy or Mealthy. Other times it’s been fundraising products like RaiseMore. Everything I work on - at it's core - is about people. Here's my CV, just in case you're curious. Also fair warning: this is where I try new things, so strap in, it's gonna get weird.

 

Some things I've learned along the way.

Design is about relationships.

Design & Business are inherently linked.

Treat design as a strategy as much as an action.

Don't take yourself too seriously.

Solving people problems often solves business problems.

Don't be a Dick.

Data & iteration are the best paths to success.
